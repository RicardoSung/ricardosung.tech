    Nov. 2020 - Aug. 2021
    • Developed a bandgap with 1.2V voltage reference and 50nA current reference, whose power consumption was less than 200nW, in the temperature variation range of -40 to 125 degrees celsius.

  5. Smartphone-based Analysis of Non-destructive Apple Brix Measurement
    Apr. 2021 - Aug. 2021
    • Designed an optical system using Zemax OpticStudio to achieve Czerny-Turner Spectroscope structure using a positive (converging) lens, two spherical mirrors, a blazed grating and a linear CCD.
    • Designed a CCD driver board including a Xilinx FPGA, a Toshiba linear CCD, a high-speed and high-precision ADC and so on to capture light spectra and transmit digital spectra data to smart phones.
    • Programmed verilog HDL codes on Xilinx FPGA to drive CCD and ADC, besides communicating with smart phones by USB protocol.
    • Won the second prize of the National University Student’s Opt-Sci-Tech Competition (2021).
  6. Intelligent Biological Microscope
    Apr. 2020 - July. 2020
    • Designed a multi-method of resizing and merging limited images under microscopes to make up a full cell image.
    • Migrated the M2Det algorithm to suit the blood cell recognition, segmentation, and counting.
    • Developed a dataset of blood cells with five classifications including neutrophils, lymphocytes, monocytes, eosinophils and basophils.
    • Won the first prize of the 8th National University Student’s Opt-Sci-Tech Competition (2020) and the first prize of China-U.S. Young Maker Competition in Chengdu Division (2020).
  7. Intelligent Device to Protect Children left in Cars
    Apr. 2019 - July. 2019
    • Designed a multi-model network merged by AgeNet and Yolo-v2 to detect children left in cars.
    • Used sensors such as temperature and humidity sensor, Oxygen sensor and AIoT to send data to private servers.
    • Programmed a mobile app to receive warnings sent by servers to remind children’s guardians of the states in cars about their children.
    • Won the third prize of the National University Student’s Opt-Sci-Tech Competition (2019).

## Project Experience

Low-power, high transient response LDO design without off-chip capacitors Major Participant    Nov. 2020 - Aug. 2021

  • Won the first prize of the National University Student Integrated Circuit Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition in Southwest Devision (2021).

Intelligent Biological Microscope Project Manager    Apr. 2020 - July. 2020

Intelligent Device to Protect Children left in Cars Project Manager    Apr. 2019 - July. 2019
